Frequently Asked Questions

What is AuxinPath?

AuxinPath is an all-in-one ABA (Applied Behavior Analysis) clinic management platform designed for BCBA and RBT teams. It combines smart scheduling, clinical documentation, data collection with real-time graphing, a caregiver portal, and HIPAA-compliant security in one modern web application.

Who is AuxinPath designed for?

AuxinPath is built for ABA therapy clinics and professionals — including BCBAs, BCaBAs, RBTs, clinic owners, practice managers, and caregivers of clients receiving ABA services.

Is AuxinPath HIPAA compliant?

Yes. AuxinPath is built with HIPAA compliance as a core requirement. It includes full audit trails for all PHI access, role-based access control with 76 granular permissions, multi-tenant data isolation, encrypted storage, and compliance dashboards for BACB and Medicaid standards.

What data collection methods are supported?

AuxinPath supports discrete trial training (DTT), task analysis, ABC (antecedent-behavior-consequence) recording, frequency counting, duration tracking, and interval recording — all with real-time progress charts and graphing.

Does AuxinPath support assessments and treatment plans?

Yes. AuxinPath includes a comprehensive 20-section integrated assessment wizard with VB-MAPP and FBA analysis, plus automatic PDF generation for Medicaid authorization requests. Assessment findings flow directly into treatment plans and behavior intervention plans.
